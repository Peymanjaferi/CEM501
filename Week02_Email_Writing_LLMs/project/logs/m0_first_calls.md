## Prompt 3
Summarize the key elements of a proper delay notice under a standard AIA contract.

### Response
A proper delay notice under a standard AIA contract typically includes the project name, the reason for the delay, the specific dates and duration of the delay, the activities affected, and a reference to the relevant contract clause. The notice should also include a request for schedule adjustment and be submitted within the timeframe required by the contract.

## Reflection

During this exercise, I observed that AI can generate structured professional communication quickly. The responses were clear and useful, especially for drafting construction-related emails. However, specific project information such as contract clauses, activity IDs, and exact dates should always be verified before using the content in a real project.
